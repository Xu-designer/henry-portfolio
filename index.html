WHO I AM

Currently I’m a design major at UC Davis. I’ve had an interest in drawing and visual design since I was a child. I was formally introduced to the field of graphic design in high school, and I want to use my expertise to help others and craft resonant messages.

WHAT I DO

Primarily, I work as a graphic designer to create anything from visual assets to full publications or posters. I use programs such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign when completing my work. I also am well-versed in UI/UX and web design, using Figma to mock up useful and accessible online websites and apps. I also have extensive experience with illustration to create visual concepts.

WHY I DESIGN

Ever since I first chose to become a designer, I’ve determined to design for the needs of others through visual communication, web-based design, and whatever design fields available to me. I believe that through effective design, we can create the greatest form of your ideas and translate them into life-changing messages and products. Let’s work together to create brilliant designs!
